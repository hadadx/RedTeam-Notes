Authentication Attacks

Authentication attacks target the mechanisms used by Active Directory to validate identities, including Kerberos, NTLM, password-based authentication, tickets, hashes, and credential material.

Authentication Attacks

  • Password spraying
  • AS-REP roasting
  • Kerberoasting
  • NTLM relay
  • Pass-the-Hash
  • Pass-the-Ticket
  • Credential dumping
